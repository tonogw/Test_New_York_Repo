About

Gunarto Wibisono is a systems and operations professional with more than two decades of experience in global banking institutions. His career began in customer service and banking operations, where daily exposure to operational inefficiencies shaped his understanding of how financial processes function in practice. Over time, his role evolved from operational management into technology and system architecture, focusing on how digital systems can transform complex financial workflows. Working across credit card services, remittance operations, treasury systems, and securities settlement infrastructure, he has consistently contributed to initiatives that replaced manual processes with integrated digital solutions. His work has included the design of straight-through transaction processing, host-to-host integration with financial market infrastructure, and operational automation initiatives within global banking environments. Today, his interests extend beyond financial systems into broader digital architecture, including enterprise platforms and IoT-enabled operational management, reflecting a continued focus on how technology can reshape organisational processes and improve efficiency.
